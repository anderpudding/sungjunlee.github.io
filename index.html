About Me

I was born in Korea and spent the first 10 years of my life there before moving to Japan. In Japan, I completed elementary school and successfully passed the rigorous "Juken" entrance exams, earning admission to the third most competitive private school in Tokyo. At the age of 14, a family trip to New York and Toronto sparked my fascination with Canada. The experience was so captivating that my family decided to move to Canada in 2019. I started my journey in Canada as a Grade 9 student and worked hard to achieve admission to the University of British Columbia, where I am now a student in the Faculty of Science. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games like League of Legends and Valorant, playing the guitar, and immersing myself in webtoons. I also have a passion for creating—whether it’s crafting or drawing, I love bringing ideas to life. Known for my positive and energetic personality, I pride myself on having boundless energy and enthusiasm that can brighten any room, even after days of hard work.
